Features

Lumina handles the entire meeting lifecycle so your team can stay present.

Recording & transcription

Lumina joins Zoom, Google Meet, and Microsoft Teams calls automatically. Speaker-labeled transcripts are ready minutes after the call ends, searchable across your whole workspace, in more than 30 languages.

AI summaries

Every meeting produces a structured recap: key decisions, open questions, blockers, and next steps — written for the people who weren't in the room.

Integrations

Where your work already lives

Push action items to Slack, sync recaps to Notion and Confluence, and attach transcripts to your CRM. Lumina fits your stack instead of replacing it.
