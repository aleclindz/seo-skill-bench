**Coverage gaps Lumina can win:** Head-to-head "Otter.ai alternatives" and "Otter.ai vs X" content — Otter ranks for its own brand but a well-built comparison page from a challenger frequently ranks alongside it.

## Competitor 2: Fireflies.ai — fireflies.ai
**Positioning:** Sales-team favorite — 50+ integrations (Salesforce, HubSpot, Slack), automated follow-ups from transcripts.
**Content focus:** CRM/sales-ops angle, integration-heavy guides.
**Coverage gaps:** Little content for general ops/team-lead audiences outside sales — Lumina's ICP (teams/ops leads generally, not just sales) is a gap.

## Competitor 3: Fathom — fathom.video
**Positioning:** "Free forever" unlimited recording/transcripts for individuals — the budget/free-tier leader.
**Content focus:** Free-tier comparison posts, individual-user angle.
**Coverage gaps:** Team/collaboration workflows (multi-seat, Slack/Notion sync) are under-covered relative to its individual-user content.

## Competitor 4: tl;dv — tldv.io
**Positioning:** Video-clip sharing + timestamped highlights, privacy-forward.
**Content focus:** Clip/sharing workflows, privacy comparisons.
**Coverage gaps:** Weak on the "decisions + action items" summary angle that's Lumina's core hook.

## Competitor 5: Fellow — fellow.app
**Positioning:** Meeting-workflow tool (agendas + notes + action items), not just a notetaker.
**Content focus:** Meeting-productivity / better-meetings content, adjacent to pure transcription.
**Coverage gaps:** Less focused on pure AI-transcription accuracy content.
